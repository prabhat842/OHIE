# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be factual, be respectful, and keep the scientific claims inside the evidence.

## Expected behavior

- critique the model, not the person
- keep discussion focused on evidence, reproducibility, and limitations
- avoid misleading claims

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment
- personal attacks
- deliberate misrepresentation of results
- pressure to overclaim beyond the evidence

## Enforcement

Maintainers may ask contributors to revise language, documentation, or code when claims exceed the available evidence.

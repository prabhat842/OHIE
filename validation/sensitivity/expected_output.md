# Expected Sensitivity Behavior

Expected trends:

- Manning changes should affect depth and extent without solver collapse.
- Coarser resolution should remain stable but may change local maxima and flooded extent.
- Larger timestep should remain stable up to the tested range; mass error should flag unsafe regions.
- Higher rainfall should increase volume and generally increase flooding.
- Larger detention basins should not produce worse total volume reduction than smaller basins under the same setup.

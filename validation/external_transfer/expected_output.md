# Expected Behavior

- the open DEM should produce a floodplain-scale response under rainfall forcing
- the flux-coupled boundary should affect inundation and persistence at the river-adjacent edge
- the default coefficient should be tested first without tuning
- if the result is ambiguous, a minimal sensitivity sweep may be used to classify the transfer behavior

Expected interpretation:

- qualitative transfer only, not calibration
- no claim that the coefficient will behave like a universal default

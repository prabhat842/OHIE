# Reviewer Readiness Checklist

Could a reviewer now answer:

- What works?
- What fails?
- Why trust anything?
- What evidence supports the claims?
- What should not be trusted?

Checklist:

- The overwrite boundary is not presented as coupling
- The flux-coupled approximation is not presented as calibrated hydraulics
- The shared-default failure is explicit
- The Brahmaputra case is explicitly partial transfer, not validation
- The terrain-regime sweep shows where transfer weakens
- The vocabulary stays inside the lock
- The evidence matrix links claims to the phase reports
- The scientific position page is the single source of truth

If any of the above is unclear, the package is not ready for external review.

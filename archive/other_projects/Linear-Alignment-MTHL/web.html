One Platform, End-to-End.

Traditional planning is slow, expensive, and reactive. Our platform turns your GIS data into optimized, physics-validated solutions in three automated stages.

01

Diagnostics

Our engine processes your raw GIS data (DEM, LULC, Soil) to create a "Cost & Suitability Atlas," identifying all risks and opportunities.

02

AI Planning

A Genetic Algorithm explores thousands of potential solutions—runway alignments, bridge paths, or flood pond layouts—evaluating each against the atlas to find the top 5 "best" options.

03

Validation

We run your top plans through our proprietary **HRF-SWE physics solver** to simulate real-world stress (like a storm surge or flood event), ensuring the final design is not just cheap, but resilient.
